Things About Ohio

  1. Ohio has two main nicknames: "The Buckeye State" and "The Birthplace of Aviation"

  2. The official state bird of Ohio is the Cardinal

  3. The official state flower of Ohio is the Red Carnation

  4. The official state insect of Ohio is the Ladybug

  5. The official state mammal of Ohio is the White-Tailed Deer

  6. The official state reptile of Ohio is the Black Racer Snake

  7. The official state tree of Ohio is of course the Buckeye tree
